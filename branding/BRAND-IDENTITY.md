## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | ANSI (256) |
|------|------|-----|-----------|
| Primary | Terminal Green | `#00FF41` | `\e[38;5;46m` |
| Background | Deep Space | `#0D1117` | `\e[38;5;232m` |
| Accent | Aurora Cyan | `#79C0FF` | `\e[38;5;117m` |
| AI Purple | Neural Purple | `#BC8CFF` | `\e[38;5;141m` |
| Warning | Amber | `#E3B341` | `\e[38;5;178m` |
| Error | Soft Red | `#F85149` | `\e[38;5;203m` |
| Text | Off-White | `#E6EDF3` | `\e[38;5;255m` |

### Palette Rationale

- **Terminal Green** — classic phosphor green; evokes the hacker/terminal
  aesthetic and the "alive" quality of an OS
- **Deep Space** — GitHub dark-mode background; familiar to developers
- **Aurora Cyan** — references the "Aurora" codename and the northern lights
- **Neural Purple** — signals AI/intelligence; high contrast on dark backgrounds
- **Amber / Red** — standard warning and error semantic colors
